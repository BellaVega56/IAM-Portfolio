**Q3.** If HR is the "master" for the `department` attribute, and you change it directly in Okta, what happens on the next HR sync?
- **My Answer:** A — Your change is kept ✗
- **Correct:** B — Your change is overwritten with HR's value
- **Lesson learned:** The master wins on EVERY sync, unconditionally — not just when the master changes.
